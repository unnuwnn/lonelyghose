FAQ

1. What is the Lonely Ghose Spirit World?

The Lonely Ghose Spirit World is an upcoming PFP social club project launching on the Solana blockchain.
A "Boo (Model 1)" NFT in your wallet will act as proof of membership to our Spirit World allowing a lifetime membership to LonelyGhose© airdrops, giveaways, and collaborations.

2. Which network did you launch on?

The Lonely Ghose have chosen to haunt and wander the blocks on the Solana chain.
We have decided to grace our ghouls with this chain because of the low gas fees and scary fast transaction speeds.

3. When is the launch? How much will it cost? How many will be availabe?

The launch date will be October 25th, 2021 @ 3PM PST.
The price of 1 “Boo (Model 1)” will be at a mint price of 2 SOL .
There will be a supply of 1,313 "Boo (Model 1)'s". The Dev wallet will hold 113 mints for future giveaways/burns/devs.

4. What secondary marketplace will the Lonely Ghose be available on after launch?

The Lonely Ghose still needs to determine which marketplace they will haunt.
Aiming for Solanart and Alpha.Art overall.

5. How many Lonely Ghose can I mint/own?

There is no limit on how many Lonely Ghose you can mint but you can only mint 1 per transaction.
There is no limit on how many Boos you can own.

6. Was there a pre-sale or whitelist?

No, The Lonely Ghose decided to try to be more fair to the investors so there will be no presale.
We will give a role for early supporters & discord invite track winners to enter a giveaway raffle of a few "Boo (Model 1)"s and they will be given away after launch.

7. Is there a rarity tool that I can use to see the rank of my Ghose?

As of now there is no rarity tool, but we plan on getting listed on a rarity website!
The rarity data will be posted on our Twitter/Discord/Website and a tool will eventually be integrated with our website.

8. Who made the art?

The art was all handmade by our amazing Founder/Developer Unnuwnn! As well as the website!

9. What are you trying to strive to achieve with this project?

We are striving to become the first "Designer NFT Project".
We hope to bring some awesome artwork and beyond to this beloved community as well as work our way into becoming one of the first Luxury/Designer NFT Brands.
